We will be holding our third annual invitational this spring. We will be hosting a Division C tournament and will be following the North Carolina state schedule and state rules. The tournament will be held on UNC Chapel Hill's campus.

Registration

Registration for our 2024 tournament opens on October 1st and will run until November 1st or until all spots are filled! You will be able to register your team through our google form which will be posted on this page when registration opens. A maximum of 30 schools and 60 teams (two teams/school) will be allowed. Additionally, 15 spots will be reserved for underserved teams. There will be a registration fee of $80 per school, and $40 for each additional team a school brings. However, fee waivers will be provided for schools that need them.
